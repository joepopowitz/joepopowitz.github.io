About This Portfolio

This is a collection of projects I've worked on across packaging, product design, and hands-on DIY engineering. For hiring managers: the most relevant work for Packaging Engineering roles is listed in the first section below.

Packaging Projects

Corrugated Hardware Storage System

Designed and produced a modular corrugated packaging system for hardware storage, enabling low-MOQ custom orders and generating $100k+ revenue. Created dieless prototypes, cost-optimized structure, and delivered full documentation.

ISTA / ASTM Testing Projects

Conducted classroom/lab testing on drop, vibration, and compression standards for packaging prototypes. Interpreted failure data to improve design performance and cushioning systems.

FlexEdge Desk Mat Concept

Modular desk mat system with swappable edge accessories (pen tray, cable guide, wrist pad). Designed to improve ergonomics and user customization for desk environments.

Product Design & Prototyping

SlideDock – Wrist Rest with Drawer

Created a hybrid wrist rest and storage solution for desks. 3D printed prototypes to validate feel and fit. Focused on solving clutter and improving comfort.

Infinity Mirror Phone Case

Designed and built a phone case that integrates a lit infinity mirror using LEDs and acrylic. Demonstrated skills in layering, fit tolerance, and visual effect design.

Pleco Breeding Cave System

CAD-modeled and 3D printed a modular cave set for aquarium fish breeding. Used spline modeling and natural shaping to simulate organic rock forms. Designed for function and easy fry access.

DIY Engineering & Systems Projects

Greenhouse Layout + Grow System

Designed a full-sun raised bed greenhouse system with a vertical trellis. Focused on airflow, drainage, and plant selection. Built and tested in-season to optimize yield and layout.

Rain Barrel + First Flush Diverter

Created a custom rainwater collection system including a self-flushing diverter for cleaner water capture. Designed for modularity, durability, and ease of use.
